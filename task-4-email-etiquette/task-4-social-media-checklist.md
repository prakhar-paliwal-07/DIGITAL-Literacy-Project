SOCIAL MEDIA DO'S AND DONT'S


DO'S---

1) THINK BEFORE POSTING ANYTHING ONLINE
2) ALWAYS KEEP YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION SAFE
3) TALK WITH RESPECT AND POLITENESS
4) ALWAYS SHARE USEFULL AND POLITE CONTENT
5) ALWAYS FACTCHECK BEFORE SHARING ANYTHING ONLINE


DONT'S---
1) DONT SHARE FAKE NEWS
2) DONT POST ANYTHING OFFESIVE
3) DONT OVERSHARE PERSONAL DETAILS
4) DONT ENGAGE IN E- FIGHTS
5) DONT OVERSPEND YOUR TIME IN SOCIAL MEDIA
