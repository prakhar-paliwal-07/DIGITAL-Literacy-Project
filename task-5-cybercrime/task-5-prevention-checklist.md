HOW TO STAY SAFE ONLINE


SAFETY TIPS----


1) ALWAYS USE STRONG PASSWORDS
2) NEVER SHARE OTP OR PERSONAL DETAILS WITH ANYONE
3) MAKE SURE TO VERIFY LINKS BEFORE CLICKING
4) ENABLE 2FA ON ALL YOUR ACCOUNTS
5) AVOID USING PUBLIC WIFI FOR YOUR BANKIG WORKS
6) ALWYAYS KEEP ALL YOUR APPS UPDATED TO THE LATEST SECURITY PATCHES

UPI/FINANCIAL SAFETY---


1) NEVER APPROVE SUSPICIOUS PAYMENTS REQUESTS ON UPI APPS LIKE PAYTM , PHONEPAY ETC
2) ALWAYS DOUBLE CHECK THE RECEIVER'S NAME AND THEIR PERSONAL DETAILS BEFORE YOU SEND THEN ANY OF YOUR PERSONAL DETAILS


HOW TO REPORT CYBERCRIMES---


1) REPORT SUCH FRAUDS IMMEDIATELY AT  https://cybercrime.gov.in
2) CALL THE CYBERCRIME HELP LINE NUMBER-1930
