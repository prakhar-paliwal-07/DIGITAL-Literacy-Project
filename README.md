🌐 Digital Literacy Project
📌 Project Overview

This project is based on Digital Literacy, which refers to the ability to use digital things , the internet and its stuff, and online platforms safely and effeicently, effectively, and responsibly without creating any mess.

It includes multiple tasks that focus on awareness of safe internet usage, email communication, digital platforms, and cybercrime. A complete project report is also included.

🎯 Objectives
To understand digital literacy and its fundaamentals
To promote safe internet practices and usage
To learn proper email etiquette withount sounding improfessioinal
To explore different digital platforms
To create awareness about cybercrime and dont get invlolved in any fraud
📂 Project Contents
1. One-Page Presentation

A short presentation explaining digital literacy, its meaning, and importance all in just one page short and simple.

2. Portfolio

A collection of all tasks and activities completed in the project including my various profiles in different platforms.

3. Different Digital Platforms

Explanation of various platforms such as social media, educational tools, and communication platforms.

4. Email Etiquette

Rules and guidelines for writing professional emails with a good tone and language.

5. Cybercrime Awareness

Information about cyber threats, types of cybercrime, and safety measures and knoledge about all the different types of threats carried around .

6. Project Report

A detailed report summarizing all the work done in the project and indepth explaination of it.
